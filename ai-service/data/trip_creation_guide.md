# How to Create a Trip on Trek Tribe

## Overview
Creating a trip on Trek Tribe is a comprehensive 7-step process that allows organizers to set up detailed adventure packages.

## Prerequisites
- You must be registered as an **Organizer** on Trek Tribe
- You must be logged in to your account
- Navigate to the "Create Adventure" section from the header menu

## The 7-Step Trip Creation Process

### Step 1: Basic Information
**What you need:**
- **Trip Title**: A catchy, descriptive name for your adventure
- **Destination**: Where the trip will take place
- **Description**: Detailed information about what the trip offers
- **Categories**: Select appropriate tags (e.g., trekking, adventure, beach, mountain, wildlife)

**Example:**
```
Title: "Himalayan Base Camp Trek"
Destination: "Nepal, Himalayas"
Description: "Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas..."
Categories: trekking, adventure, mountains
```

### Step 2: Dates & Pricing
**What you need:**
- **Start Date**: When the trip begins
- **End Date**: When the trip concludes
- **Price**: Cost per person (in ₹ Rupees)
- **Capacity**: Maximum number of participants

**Important:** 
- Dates must be in the future
- Price should cover all inclusions mentioned
- Capacity helps manage bookings

### Step 3: Itinerary/Schedule
**What you need:**
Create a day-by-day schedule with:
- **Day Number**: Day 1, Day 2, etc.
- **Title**: Brief title for the day (e.g., "Arrival in Kathmandu")
- **Activities**: List of activities planned for that day

**Example:**
```
Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu
- Airport pickup
- Hotel check-in
- Welcome dinner
- Trip briefing

Day 2: Trek to Base Camp
- Morning breakfast
- Start trek at 7 AM
- Reach base camp by afternoon
- Evening campfire
```

### Step 4: Images & Media
**What you need:**
- **Cover Image**: Main image representing your trip
- **Gallery Images**: Multiple photos (3-10 recommended)
- Upload high-quality images showing the destination, activities, accommodations

**File Requirements:**
- Formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP
- Maximum size: 5MB per image
- Recommended resolution: 1920x1080 or higher

### Step 5: Inclusions & Exclusions
**What you need:**
- **Inclusions**: What's covered in the price
  - Accommodation
  - Meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  - Transportation
  - Guide services
  - Entry fees
  - Equipment

- **Exclusions**: What participants need to pay separately
  - Personal expenses
  - Travel insurance
  - Optional activities
  - Tips for guides

**Example:**
```
Inclusions:
✓ 6 nights accommodation (tents/lodges)
✓ All meals during trek
✓ Professional trekking guide
✓ Porter services
✓ Permits and entry fees
✓ First aid kit

Exclusions:
✗ Travel to/from starting point
✗ Travel insurance
✗ Personal trekking gear
✗ Emergency evacuation
✗ Tips for guide and porters
```

### Step 6: Terms & Policies
**What you need:**
- **Cancellation Policy**: Refund terms
  - Full refund: 30+ days before trip
  - 50% refund: 15-30 days before
  - No refund: Less than 15 days
  
- **Terms & Conditions**: Important rules
  - Minimum age requirements
  - Fitness level required
  - Safety guidelines
  - Liability clauses

- **Important Notes**:
  - Weather-dependent activities
  - Required documents (ID, permits)
  - Health requirements
  - Emergency contact information

### Step 7: Review & Publish
**Final checks:**
- Review all information for accuracy
- Preview how the trip will appear to users
- Verify pricing and dates
- Ensure all images are uploaded
- Click "Publish Trip" to make it live

**After Publishing:**
- Trip appears in the marketplace immediately
- Users can view and book your trip
- You'll receive notifications for new bookings
- You can edit the trip anytime from your dashboard

## Navigation & Access
**How to get there:**
1. Click on **"+ Create Adventure"** in the header (visible only to organizers)
2. Or navigate to: `/create-trip` route
3. Or go to your Organizer Dashboard → "Create New Trip"

**Route Path:** `/create-trip`

## Tips for Success
1. **Use high-quality images**: First impressions matter
2. **Be detailed**: More information = more bookings
3. **Price competitively**: Research similar trips
4. **Clear itinerary**: Help travelers visualize the experience
5. **Transparent policies**: Build trust with clear terms
6. **Regular updates**: Keep trip information current

## Managing Your Trips
**After creation, you can:**
- View all your trips in the Organizer Dashboard
- Edit trip details anytime
- View bookings and participant lists
- Communicate with participants
- Update availability
- Cancel or modify dates

**Dashboard Route:** `/organizer/crm` or click "My Bookings" → "CRM Dashboard"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Who can create trips?**
A: Only users registered as "Organizers" can create trips.

**Q: Can I edit a trip after publishing?**
A: Yes, go to your trips list and click "Edit" on any trip.

**Q: How do I handle bookings?**
A: All bookings appear in your CRM dashboard with participant details.

**Q: What if I need to cancel a trip?**
A: You can update the trip status to "cancelled" from the edit page.

**Q: How do participants pay?**
A: Payment is handled through Razorpay integration during booking.

**Q: Can I create multiple trips?**
A: Yes, based on your subscription plan (5-50 trips depending on tier).
